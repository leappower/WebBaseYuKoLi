Our Story

Founded in 2004 in Foshan, Guangdong, YuKoLi started as a small food processing plant. We have always focused on health food R&D and manufacturing, providing professional OEM/ODM services for global brands.

After 20 years, YuKoLi has grown into a health food manufacturer with 4 modern factories and 7 product lines. We hold HACCP/FDA/ISO certifications, exporting to 30+ countries.
